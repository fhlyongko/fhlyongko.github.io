About

Academic Profile

Yong-Jik Lee is a Professor in the College of Sarim Honors at Changwon National University. His teaching and research focus on the intersection of artificial intelligence, English education, and higher education innovation.

Research Identity

AI-Mediated Learning

His work examines how students understand, adopt, and learn with AI tools in university learning contexts.

Teaching Context

English and Academic Communication

He connects AI literacy with EFL learning, academic English writing, and English-Medium Instruction.

Institutional Role

Higher Education Innovation

His scholarship asks what universities must do to support responsible and pedagogically meaningful AI use.

Research

Research Areas

The research program is organized around AI literacy, generative AI for language learning, and academic communication in higher education.

Area 01

AI Literacy in Higher Education

University students' knowledge, attitudes, competencies, and learning challenges around AI across academic disciplines and learner groups.

AI LiteracyHigher EducationStudent Competency
Area 02

Generative AI in Language Learning

Use and perception of ChatGPT, machine translation, and generative AI tools in EFL contexts, with attention to instructional design.

Generative AIEFLTEFL
Area 03

EMI and Academic Communication

Student-faculty communication in English-Medium Instruction contexts, including pragmatics, formality, and academic correspondence.
