About

Academic Profile

Yong-Jik Lee is a Professor in the College of Sarim Honors at Changwon National University. His teaching and research focus on artificial intelligence, English education, and higher education innovation.

Research Identity

AI-Mediated Learning

His work examines how students understand, adopt, and learn with AI tools in university learning contexts.

Teaching Context

English and Academic Communication

He connects AI literacy with EFL learning, academic English writing, and English-Medium Instruction.

Institutional Role

Higher Education Innovation

His scholarship asks what universities must do to support responsible and pedagogically meaningful AI use.

Current Academic Focus

The academic profile is built around responsible AI use, student-centered English learning, and evidence-based curriculum design for universities.
